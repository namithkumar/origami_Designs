Interesting Facts About Chameleons

    Chamelons are reptiles that are a part of the iguana suborder. They can change their skin colour which is an important way of communication among Chameleons. They have a long sticky tongue to catch insects. They have along tail which they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

    They are incredibly complex and smart birds. They have outstanding navigational abilities. They have excellent hearing abilities too. They are highly sociable animals.
